Fast spread of the COVID-19 pandemic has raised huge challenges for global healthcare systems .And also food,medicine delivery and monitoring patient health condition is huge challange face by health workers.The most spreading of this pandemic is due to the direct contact with an infected patient. The virus that causes covid 19 is mainly transmitted through droplets generated when an infected person coughs, sneezes or exhales.

At this time we are introducing a robotic medical assistant called "COVID-19 PATIENT AID".The robots can be deployed in isolation wards for COVID-19 infected cases for delivery of food and medicine to patients, Monitoring health, and collection of contagious waste . The robot distributes medicines and foods to COVID-19 patients inside a COVID-19 ward and disinfects themselves before returning to a health worker who stays in a green zone. The health worker can control the robot from a safe distance and see real-time data on the PATIENT AID'S WEBSITE . The robot is equipped with multi-sensors With a large-capacity tray, the bot can deliver lots of medicines, meals, and other supplies to patients in the hospital to reduce the burden on the medical staff. An advantage of it is that the health workers can avoid going to red zones wearing PPE kits in a Covid treatment centre. After serving the patients foods and medicine, the robot can be disinfected inside a tend through a fogging method




WHY PATIENT AID?

The most spreading of this pandemic is due to the direct contact with an infected patient. The virus that causes covid 19 is mainly transmitted through droplets generated when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or exhales. Using our robot, the direct contact of medical practitioners and covid 19 isolated patients can be neglected. The major features of this device are pulse and temperature monitoring of patients, delivering food and drugs to the patients, providing sanitizer, and also health care workers can see patient pulse rate and temperature in patient aide web dashboard. A large number of hospitals are seeking the help of Robotics out of an urgent need for non-contact delivery and health monitoring of covid patients. They can reduce contact between people and effectively prevents the spread of the virus. And also reduce the cost of purchasing PPE kits.


HOW IT'S WORKS?

It is a remote control robot and its movement controlled by a remote,and the 2 sensors , Pulse Sensor and LM35 Temperature Sensors measure BPM & Temperature respectively. The Arduino processes the code and displays it to LCD Display. ESP8266 Wi-Fi module connects to Wi-Fi and sends the data to patient aide server.Finally, the data can be monitored from any part of the world by logging into the patient aid website.
